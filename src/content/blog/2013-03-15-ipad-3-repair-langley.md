I completed the repair of a White iPad 3 that a coworker brought me. The screen was broken from after the iPad was left on a Hockey net where the kids were shooting pucks at!!!<br />
<br />
The iPad turned out great. I even took the time to shoot a Time Lapse that I will be posting shortly. I did have to do some body work on one of the corners as it had a small dent that would have prevented the new screen to go in properly.  <br />
