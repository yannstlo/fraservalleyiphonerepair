After repairing a customer's iPhone a few weeks back, they contacted me asking to help with a Toshiba Laptop with a broken LCD. As I don't advertise the service I was pleased that they contacted me for help. <br />
<br />
Since I do not carry laptop parts I had to find a good and reliable supplier. I went with http://laptopscreen.com/. The price was right and the new LCD was delivered in 2 days.<br />
<br />
I picked up the laptop from the customer tonight and once the kids were in bed, went to work on it. I have replaced an LCD on a Dell Laptop in the past and had to take the entire unit apart, this one was much simpler. <br />
