I help a small business owner tonight with his iPad 3 that had a broken screen. I had recently visited his coffee shop to meet an Aldergrove resident that needed an iPhone 4S repaired. While I was repairing the iPhone, the business owner inquired if I could help him with his iPad. He wasn't prepared to let it go that evening but suggested that he would call when business was and he could be without his iPad for the night.<br />
<br />
I picked up the iPad around 5 today, repaired it at home and will be retuning it to its owner tomorrow.<br />
