I had 2 customers with identical issues on their iPhones 4S today. Both of them had dropped their iPhones and the glass of the screens were not broken but the image on the LCD was only showing coloured lines, one left and right, and the other, up and down. If you ever encounter this, it can be fixed by replacing the front screen assembly.<br />
