Yes, I do offer home button replacement&nbsp;but here is a quick solution that might hold you up for a few days if your home button is failing on you.<br />
<br />
&nbsp;With the venue of iOS 5, Apple has added many new features under the Accessibility Setting Section. One of which allows you to add a button over the screen that gives you shortcuts to many options one if which being the home button.<br />
