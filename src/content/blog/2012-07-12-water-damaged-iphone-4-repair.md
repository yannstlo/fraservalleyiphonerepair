i have just finished repairing a water damaged iPhone 4. The phone would not start up on its own and needed to be connected to a power outlet to fully boot. Sounded like a power issue and it was. I replaced the battery with a new one and solved the problem.<br />
<br />
My Chilliwack customer will be very pleased that his water damaged iPhone that went for a swim is now 100% functional again.<br />
<br />
Lucky him, the motherboard is intact.<br />
